I'm Valdigleis, a Flamengo fan, former programmer, and now an adjunct professor of mathematics «Erdös number 4» and computer science «Turing number 7», currently working at CCICOMP of the Federal University of São Francisco Valley. I obtained my PhD title in systems and computing «field: mathematical fundamentals of computing» from PPgSC at the Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte under the supervision of professors Benjamin Bedregal and Regivan Santiago.

I have been working mostly on formal languages and automata theory, specifically on non-classical automata models. So far, I have extended the automata models for typical hesitant fuzzy models. Other interests in «abstract» mathematics include {type, category, set, proof, graph, numbers} theory, and, moreover, in «applied» mathematics, I'm interested in formal software specification, cryptography, neural networks, and signal processing.
